Our ALUMNI

The Debating Society, NIT Durgapur, is an ardent believer of a wholesome development where students would actively involve themselves in issues concerning social interest and upsurge. As responsible citizens, we feel the importance to amplify voices when the nation requires our support and opinions. We look forward to participation from the youth wing with solutions to the massive lockjaws with intellect, humanity and feasibility. Each consecutive step of our club attempts to bring a stir towards positive outcomes in the society and provide a platform to creative ideologies piloting progress. We believe the youth of the hour can bring a change towards positivity and stability only when we are vigilant, receptive and equally responsive in raising our voices and directing our thought processes towards the present issues concerning the society.
