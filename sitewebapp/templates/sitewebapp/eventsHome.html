Our Events

The Debating Society focuses on events that are sure to enrich and instil the passion of debate and discussion in students and allow them to present their ideas on a public forum. We aim to provide a platform for people to voice their opinions and to encourage more people to start meaningful discussions. Some of our most ambitious projects till date would be hosting the first edition of the Asian Parliamentary Debate in NIT Durgapur and conducting Kartavya, [more details about Kartavya]. We also regularly hold events like Open Mic and our flagship event Reverberate, A Public Speaking Workshop where we offer pointers and tips to students who want to begin their journey in the public speaking forum.
